Why should we recycle e-waste?


  1. It is very important to keep electronic waste out of landfills.

  2. Electronic products are comprised of valuable materials such as precious metals like gold, silver and platinum along with copper, aluminum, plastic and glass.

  3. Reclaiming valuable materials from the recycling process means there will be decreased demand for new raw materials.
